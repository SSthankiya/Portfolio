Android 6.0 Marshmallow is finally here

Google has finally brought out the latest android update to their Nexus, Android 6.0 Marshmallow. Android 6.0 will be available for Nexus 5, Nexus 6, Nexus 7, Nexus 9 and Nexus Player. This new update is mainly focusing on a wide variety of improvement to the phone for example better battery life through the usage of the new feature doze, completely revamp of the app permission. Google have also added a new feature called Now on Tap which might be the biggest user feature within Android 6.0.
